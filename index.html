Why Hire Me

I am a qualified lawyer, a CAMS-certified AML specialist, and a holder of an LLM in International Banking Law and Finance from the University of Edinburgh. That combination was built deliberately: to understand financial crime as a legal, regulatory, operational, and systemic issue.

Many professionals come from one direction only — law, finance, audit, or operations. I bring exposure across multiple disciplines, allowing me to understand not only what a rule says, but how it should work in practice.

I specialise in areas where technical depth matters: transaction monitoring, sanctions screening, alert logic, customer risk assessment, and regulatory expectations.

I understand the architecture behind CDD, EDD, PEP classification, suspicious reporting thresholds, and governance expectations. I can distinguish between compliance that merely looks adequate and compliance that can withstand scrutiny.

If you need someone to simply process alerts, there are many options. If you need someone who can think critically about why the alerts exist, how systems should improve, and how regulation translates into operations, I am worth speaking to.
